Other Approaches
Most owners try to exit on their own — negotiating directly with the resort, listing it for sale, or hiring an individual exit company. None of these approaches carry the weight of a coordinated group filing.
Why a Group Filing Is Different
When dozens of owners document the same violations against the same resort, the resort faces a consolidated legal and financial exposure they cannot ignore.
