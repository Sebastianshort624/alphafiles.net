Why This Assessment Matters

Every violation you document here contributes to a shared case file with other owners from the same resort. You are not filing alone. Multiple participants documenting the same violations creates a legally recognized behavioral pattern — the foundation of a group filing.

Our software calculates the resort's total financial exposure across all participants. That number — often in the millions of dollars — is what we present when we negotiate your release. The more thoroughly this is documented, the stronger the leverage for everyone in your group.

Each violation below carries a specific fine under federal or state law. If we report these violations to the appropriate enforcement agencies, the resort faces those fines directly.
