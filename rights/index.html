Why This Assessment Matters

Every violation documented here contributes to a shared case file with other owners from the same resort. Multiple participants documenting the same violations creates a legally recognized behavioral pattern — the foundation of a group filing.

Our software calculates the resort's total financial exposure across all participants. That number — often in the millions of dollars — is what we present when we negotiate your release.
