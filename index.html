Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Are you attorneys?
No – we’re a consumer advocacy company specializing in timeshare cancellation. Our unique approach allows us to provide written guarantees that attorneys legally cannot offer. If your case ever requires legal intervention, we have attorneys on retainer who step in at no additional cost.
Do I have to pay anything upfront?
No. You can get started with $0 down. Your first payment isn’t due until your second month of service. We also offer a discount if you prefer to pay in full upfront.
What happens to my credit?
If you decide to change your payment approach during the process, any credit impact is typically temporary and repairable. Our complimentary in-house Credit Solutions Team assists with any timeshare-related derogatory marks across all three bureaus. We can’t advise you to stop payments — that’s always your decision.
What makes you different from other companies?
Three main things: Our proprietary group filing method (we’re the only firm that does this), our 100% in-house operation (nothing is outsourced), and our Protection Release approach (we pursue clean legal releases, not managed foreclosures that damage your credit).
