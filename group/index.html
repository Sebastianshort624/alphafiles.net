One case is a complaint.
Fifty is a pattern.
When multiple owners from the same resort document the same violations, it becomes a group filing. The resort faces consolidated legal and financial exposure they cannot ignore.
How It Works
Pattern Recognition
Multiple clients reveal repeat sales behavior. Individual cases become system-level evidence — shared violations become documented behavioral patterns.
Exposure Multiplication
When 50 contracts show the same violations simultaneously, the math forces resolution. The resort faces millions in potential fines — all at once.
Timeline Advantage
Individual cases drag for years. Group filings typically resolve in 6–10 months because the resort cannot ignore the consolidated pressure.
Compensation
In some filings, 20–40% of participants receive compensation checks. These come directly from the resort to you. Alpha keeps zero — no percentage, no cut.
Why the Resort Settles
It is because of group filings that we see resorts settle — and sometimes pay compensation. The resort must grant exit to all participants, not pick and choose. This is what makes it work.

This is the leverage.
