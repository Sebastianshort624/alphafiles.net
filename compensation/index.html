06
Client Impact & Damages Documentation
Non-financial harm assessment — document in client's own words for attorney review
The impact of a timeshare dispute isn't only measured in dollars. Attorneys need your story — how this affected your credit, your peace of mind, your daily life, and your future. Select each area that applies, then document in the client's own words.
Emotional Distress
How has owning this timeshare made you feel over time?
↳ "Tell me more about that. How long have you felt this way?"
Stress & Anxiety
Has this situation caused you stress or anxiety? Have you ever lost sleep over it?
↳ "How often? What does that feel like?"
Loss of Peace of Mind
Has this been something that's constantly in the back of your mind? Has it affected your overall peace of mind?
↳ "What has been the hardest part of dealing with this?"
Feeling Trapped
Do you feel trapped or stuck in this contract? Why?
↳ "How has that feeling affected your daily life?"
Credit Damage
How has this situation affected your credit score or your ability to borrow money? Have you been denied anything because of this?
↳ "Can you describe what that was like for you?"
Collection & Harassment
Have you continued receiving collection calls, letters, or notices even after you stopped engaging?
↳ "How often? How has dealing with their contact affected your day-to-day life?"
Fear of Foreclosure
How concerned are you about them forcing foreclosure or taking further legal action?
↳ "What worries you most about where this could lead if it stays unresolved?"
Family & Lifestyle Impact
Has this limited your ability to spend money on family or personal priorities? Has it prevented vacations you wanted to take?
↳ "How did your family experience this with you?"
Regret Over Purchase
If you had known what you know now, would you have signed? Did you feel fully informed at the time?
↳ "What were you told that turned out not to be true?"
Sales Misrepresentation
Were you told anything during the sales process that turned out not to be accurate? Did you feel pressured or rushed to sign?
↳ "Can you describe exactly what was said to you?"
Heir & Estate Concern
Have you thought about what happens to this obligation as you get older or what it means for your heirs?
↳ "How does it feel knowing this could be passed down to your family?"
Time & Energy Cost
How much time and energy have you spent dealing with this resort — calls, disputes, paperwork, worrying?
↳ "How long has this been impacting you? What would you have done with that time?"
