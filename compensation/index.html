How Compensation Becomes Part of a Settlement

When the documented financial exposure exceeds the cost of resolving the matter, many resorts determine that providing compensation is simply less expensive than continuing the dispute.

Compensation is not automatic, and every settlement is different. However, some agreements include reimbursement or monetary consideration in addition to releasing the owner from the timeshare entirely.

What to Know About Compensation
✅ When It Happens
When documented exposure is significant enough that the resort concludes compensation is less costly than continued legal defense or regulatory risk.
⚠️ What We Don't Promise
Compensation is not guaranteed in every case. Every settlement is independently negotiated. We never promise a specific outcome beyond what is in your signed contract.
What is in your contract is our guarantee.
Regardless of compensation, every Alpha enrollment includes a written money-back guarantee — if we do not obtain a Favorable Outcome within 36 months, you receive a full refund of your service fee. That commitment is in writing, signed by us, in your contract.
