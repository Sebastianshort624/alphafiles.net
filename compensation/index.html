Who Qualifies for Higher Compensation

Amount Paid In
Owners who paid more — especially at high interest rates — have stronger compensation claims. Total principal plus interest paid is the baseline.

Time paid without use is direct evidence of financial harm. Longer ownership with minimal use means stronger recovery potential.
⚖️
Violation Count
More documented violations = more fine exposure for the resort = stronger motivation to compensate rather than litigate.
👥
Group Strength
The more participants in your resort's filing, the greater the combined exposure. Compensation frequency increases with group size.
