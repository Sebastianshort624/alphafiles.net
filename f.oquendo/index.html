What Happens to Your Brother

This is the part of timeshare ownership that is almost never explained at purchase — and the most important reason to act now.

When You Pass Away — Without an Exit
  • Your RCI membership automatically passes to your named heir
  • Your brother inherits your maintenance fee obligation — $600/yr, growing 8% per year — whether he wants it or not
  • If he doesn't formally disclaim the inheritance within 9 months (IRC §2518), he is legally treated as having accepted it
  • RCI has a documented practice of delaying HOA foreclosure on elderly owners so that the debt passes to heirs — then restarting collections fresh
