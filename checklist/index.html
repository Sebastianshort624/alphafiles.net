Third-Party / Not the Resort

Alpha Timeshare Consultants is a third-party consumer advocacy company. Alpha does not work for, represent, or partner with any resort or developer.

2

Not a Law Firm / No Legal Advice

Alpha Timeshare Consultants is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. Their roles are advocacy, coordination, and documentation support.

3

Independent Attorneys (If Needed)

In some situations, independent attorneys may be involved if legal services are needed. If that happens, they are retained separately and governed by their own agreements.

4

Paid-Off Owner Internal Options (Skip if Not Applicable)

If my timeshare is paid off, I understand some resorts may offer internal surrender or exit programs and Alpha will advise me of those options.

5

No Sale or Transfer

Alpha Timeshare Consultants does not buy, sell, rent, or transfer my timeshare to a new owner. Alpha pursues a direct release from the developer.

6

No Payment Advice / No Coercion

Alpha Timeshare Consultants cannot advise me to stop making payments to my resort or lender. I acknowledge all payment decisions are my own.

7

My Participation / Letters

I understand the process requires my participation. This includes reviewing and providing documents, letters received from the resort, and responding to requests from my team.

8

Normal Pushback

I understand that during the process I may receive negative responses from resorts or agencies that are routine and expected — my team will handle them.

9

Credit Solutions Program

Alpha Timeshare Consultants is not a credit company and does not guarantee credit results. If credit solutions services are provided, they are complimentary.

10

Reimbursement

I understand reimbursement is not guaranteed. In some cases it may be possible, but it is never promised and cannot be relied upon as an expected outcome.

11

Timeline + Refund Guarantee

I understand the process may take up to the full Guarantee Term, although many matters resolve within 6–18 months. If no Favorable Outcome within the Guarantee Term, I may request a full refund or extend at no additional cost.

12

Financing / Credit Pull (If Financing Is Discussed)

If I choose to finance, I understand the lender will run a credit check to determine creditworthiness.
