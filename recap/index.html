Here is exactly what happens after you sign — no surprises, no hidden steps.
Your Path to Exit — Step by Step
1

Case Assignment

Your file is assigned to a dedicated Case Manager and Personal Advocate — not a call center. You get real people who know your file.

2

Document Coordination

Your Document Coordinator contacts you to gather what is needed to build your case file. Simple, guided process.

3

Group Filing Placement

Your case is placed into the next Collective Filing for your resort. Your violations are presented alongside other documented clients from the same developer.

4

Your Team Negotiates — You Stay Out of It

All communications with the resort and developer are handled by your team. You will not need to engage directly with anyone.

5

Written Release Confirmed

A written Mutual Release is obtained. Your name is removed from the county deed. Every financial obligation is fully terminated.
