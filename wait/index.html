The Resort Will Not Help You Exit
The same company that misrepresented this ownership to you seldom offers any way out — and never if payments are behind. What they do is collect.
What Happens If You Do Nothing
1
Calls, Letters & Immediate Credit Hit
Automated collections begin within days of a missed payment. All three credit bureaus are notified within 30 days. Most owners see a 100–150 point credit drop almost immediately.
2
Two Paths — Based on Age
Retirement Age (55+)
Resorts skip formal foreclosure intentionally — they wait. When you pass, your heirs inherit the obligation automatically.
Under 55
Full foreclosure proceeds. Stays on credit for 10 years — worse than most bankruptcies in practice.
3
Deficiency Judgment — $10k to $40k+
They return to court for all future maintenance fees owed for the life of the contract. Judges grant these routinely. It shows on credit separately as an active civil judgment.
4
No Exit Company Will Take the Case
Once active foreclosure or judgment is on record, virtually no timeshare exit firm — including Alpha — can accept the case. The clean exit window closes before this point.
5
Wage Garnishment & Property Liens
With a judgment in hand, resort attorneys move to garnish wages and file liens against your home and property. You cannot sell or refinance anything with an active lien.
✅ The Exit Window Is Still Open
A clean, documented release through a group filing removes all legal standing the resort has to pursue you — permanently. This window does not stay open forever.
