Your Credit — And Why It Stays Protected

One of the most common questions we hear is whether this process could affect your credit. We want to answer that clearly, because protecting your credit is not an afterthought — it is a core part of what Alpha provides from the moment you enroll.

1
Alpha does not tell you to stop paying. That decision is always yours.
Our agreement is explicit: any decision about your resort maintenance fees or obligations remains entirely at your independent discretion. We do not advise, instruct, or encourage stopping payments. What we do is stand beside you with a full protection team — so that regardless of what happens during the process, you are not navigating it alone.
2
Credit protection is active from day one of your enrollment.
The moment you enroll with Alpha, our in-house credit team begins monitoring your credit profile. If any timeshare-related item appears on your report during the exit process, we document it, dispute it, and submit directly to all three bureaus — TransUnion, Equifax, and Experian — at no additional cost. You do not have to manage this on your own. That is why this protection is worth enrolling in now, not after something happens.
3
When it's all said and done — your credit is restored.
Upon your Mutual Release — the signed, recorded document that removes your name from the deed permanently — Alpha submits full documentation to all three credit bureaus confirming the resolution. Any timeshare-related marks associated with the process are addressed and cleared. You exit the timeshare, and you exit with your credit intact.
