01

We use group filings. No one else does this at scale.

Every other exit company handles cases one at a time. We consolidate multiple owners from the same resort into a single, coordinated filing — presenting their violations together as a documented behavioral pattern, not isolated complaints.

When 50 contracts show the same misrepresentations, the resort is not looking at one unhappy owner. They are looking at consolidated legal and financial exposure that forces them to the table. One case is a complaint. Fifty is a pattern — and patterns are what drive resolution.

Individual cases can drag for years. Group filings typically resolve in 6–10 months because the math demands it. And only clients inside a group filing structure are typically eligible for reimbursement consideration — individual cases almost never result in any recoupment.
