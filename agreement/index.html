Definitions

In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires, the following expressions have the following meanings:

"Alpha" refers to Alpha Timeshare Consultants Inc., a Florida Corporation involved in providing timeshare consulting services.

"Client(s)" refers to the individual(s) named in the Client Information section of this Agreement who are seeking services from Alpha.

"Contractual Obligations" refers to the legally binding commitments between the client(s) and providers of timeshare, vacation ownership, or vacation club memberships.

"Favorable Outcome" is defined as a written, verifiable resolution from the timeshare developer/resort (or its authorized agent) that releases the Client from ongoing contractual or payment obligations for the ownership(s)/membership(s) listed in this Agreement.

A Favorable Outcome may include cancellation, surrender, termination, settlement, relinquishment, or other documented release of Contractual Obligations. A Favorable Outcome does not include mere submission of correspondence, complaints, disputes, or requests without the written resolution described above.

"Recoupment of Funds" refers to the recovery of some or all of the funds invested into the client's timeshare(s), vacation ownership(s), or vacation club membership(s).

"Credit Protection Services" refers to a service designed to help clients navigate personal credit protection procedures.

"Service Fee" is the amount charged by Alpha for the services provided under this Agreement, as stated in the Agreement.

"Term" refers to the duration of the contract, not exceeding 36 months, within which Alpha agrees to provide services to the client(s).

"Refund Policy" outlines the conditions under which client(s) are eligible for a refund of fees paid.

"Payment Options" refers to the different methods available to the client(s) to pay the service fee, including Credit, Debit Card, or financing through lending partners.

"Group Filings" refers to situations where Alpha, at its discretion, may coordinate certain advocacy communications for multiple clients collectively for administrative efficiency. Participation does not guarantee any particular outcome.

"Case Manager," "Personal Advocate," and "Document Coordinator" are roles assigned to individuals who guide the Client through the process and coordinate required documentation and communications in support of seeking a Favorable Outcome.

Agreement for Services

This Agreement, effective as of   (the "Effective Date"), is entered into by and between Alpha Timeshare Consultants, Inc., a Florida corporation ("Alpha"), and the undersigned client(s) (the "Client"), who reside at the address provided below. Alpha may utilize independent contractors vendors to assist with administrative, documentation, and case-management tasks. Unless expressly identified and separately signed, no third-party vendor is a contracting party to this Agreement.

1. Scope of Service

Alpha agrees to provide consumer advocacy and support services intended to help the Client seek a Favorable Outcome (as defined above) with respect to the Client's identified timeshare(s), vacation ownership(s), or vacation club membership(s). Alpha does not buy, sell, or transfer timeshare interests, does not provide buyers or transferees, and does not perform deed-transfer services for a fee.

Services include gathering, maintaining, and utilizing documents and information provided by the Client to construct a detailed case file and to support communications, disputes, and other advocacy efforts related to the Client's Contractual Obligations.

If applicable, Alpha will mentor and guide the Client through specific steps that the Client must follow to facilitate progress within a reasonable timeframe. With the Client's cooperation, Alpha may draft or assist with preparation of certain documents and may prepare the Client for potential communications with resorts, agencies, lenders, or other entities. The Client commits to complying with reasonable requests for forms, documents, and instructions. The Client will not be asked to participate in any unlawful or unethical activities.

2. Legal Disclaimer

Alpha does not offer legal services or representation and operates as a consumer advocacy firm assisting clients with timeshare-related concerns. Alpha does not provide legal advice, does not act as a law firm, and does not represent the Client in court.

3. Sole Decision on Resort Dues/Fees

Client acknowledges and agrees that this Agreement does not change Client's existing financial obligations to any resort, developer, lender, or related party. Alpha Timeshare Consultants does not advise, instruct, or encourage Client to stop making payments, withhold payments, or default on any timeshare-related dues, fees, or loan obligations. Any decision by Client to continue or discontinue payments is made solely at Client's independent discretion and is not the result of any direction, coercion, or provocation by Alpha. Client agrees Alpha is not responsible for the consequences of Client's independent payment decisions.

4. Recoupment of Funds

Any recoupment or reimbursement of funds are not guaranteed and depend on the actions and decisions of third parties. If any reimbursement is obtained from a developer/resort, it will be paid to the Client directly from the developer. Client acknowledges that Alpha does not take any commissions or proceeds from any developer reimbursements. Client also acknowledges that Alpha is not held liable if reimbursement from a developer is not obtained.

5. Group Filings (If Applicable)

If applicable, Client acknowledges that Alpha may submit certain advocacy communications individually and/or as part of a consolidated submission for administrative efficiency and timing. Consolidated submissions are not resale or transfer services and do not involve providing buyers or transferees. Client understands that participation does not guarantee reimbursement or any specific outcome, and any resolution depends on third-party decisions.

6. Credit Support and Monitoring Services

As an added benefit to our clients, Alpha Timeshare Consultants provides complimentary credit guidance and monitoring support through our in-house credit team. These services are offered at no additional cost and are designed to help clients restore and protect their credit profiles following the resolution of their timeshare obligations. Credit-related assistance may include:

  • Guidance on addressing inaccurate or outdated items related to timeshare accounts.
  • Support in preparing and submitting dispute documentation, as applicable.
  • Ongoing monitoring and education to help maintain healthy credit habits.

Alpha Timeshare Consultants is not a credit repair organization as defined under the federal Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA) and does not guarantee any specific results, including credit score changes or removals. These services are educational and advocacy-based, provided solely as a courtesy to assist clients post-resolution.

Clients who wish to participate will be enrolled after contract completion unless they choose to opt out.

7. Term and Termination of Contract

Alpha will continue to provide services intended to help the Client seek a Favorable Outcome for a period not to exceed thirty-six (36) months from the Effective Date (the "Term"), subject to the Client's reasonable cooperation as described in this Agreement.

8. Warranty and Refund Policy

Except as expressly provided in the Certificate of Guarantee and Refund Policy below, payments made to Alpha are for services performed and are non-refundable during the Term. If the Client elects to pursue third-party services independently during the Term, Alpha's obligations under this Agreement may be affected, and the Client should notify Alpha in writing so Alpha can evaluate whether continued work is reasonably possible. If no Favorable Outcome is achieved within the Term, the Client may elect either: (a) to request a full refund of the Service Fee paid to Alpha, or (b) to extend the Agreement at no additional cost for continued work. To request a refund, the Client must provide written notice to Alpha within thirty (30) days after the end of the Term. A signed release form will be required to process a refund.

9. Client's Commitment

The Client agrees to actively cooperate and maintain open communication with their assigned case manager and support personnel for the duration of the Term. If the Client chooses to terminate this Agreement before the end of the Term, Alpha will stop work upon receipt of written notice, and amounts paid will remain non-refundable except as required by law or as expressly provided in the Certificate of Guarantee. The Client acknowledges that the 100% money-back guarantee applies only after the completion of the Term and only if the Client has reasonably cooperated with Alpha's requests for documents and participation needed to pursue the matter.

10. Email & Correspondence Authorization

The Client authorizes Alpha Timeshare Consultants, Inc. ("Alpha") to receive, review, draft, and send written correspondence related to the Client's timeshare ownership, including email communications with resorts, developers, management companies, or related third parties, using a designated correspondence email address. The Client understands and agrees that such correspondence may occur without the Client's direct participation in each individual communication. This authorization is limited to correspondence only and does not transfer ownership, decision-making authority, or control to Alpha. This authorization may be revoked by the Client at any time by providing written notice to Alpha.

11. Dispute Resolution

The Client agrees that all disputes shall be resolved via non-binding mediation in Orange County, Florida, or, in the event mediation is unsuccessful, shall be resolved via binding arbitration in Orange County, Florida. The Client knowingly and voluntarily waives any and all rights to a jury trial or other formal resolution in the state or federal courts.
